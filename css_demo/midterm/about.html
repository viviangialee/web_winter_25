Hailey Bieber

All About Hailey Bieber

Early Life and Background

Hailey Bieber was born on November 22, 1996, in Tucson, Arizona. She is the daughter of actor Stephen Baldwin and graphic designer Kennya Baldwin.

Modeling Career

Hailey started modeling with Ford Models and later signed with IMG. She debuted on the runway in 2014 and has worked with brands like Ralph Lauren, Tommy Hilfiger, and Versace.

Marriage

In 2018, Hailey married Justin Bieber. They had a courthouse ceremony followed by a bigger wedding in South Carolina.

Rhode Skincare

Hailey launched her skincare brand, Rhode, in 2022. It focuses on affordable, simple, and effective products.

Style Icon

Known for her "clean girl aesthetic," Hailey has set trends like glazed donut nails and dewy makeup.

Fun Fact

Hailey trained as a ballerina before modeling but stopped due to an injury.
